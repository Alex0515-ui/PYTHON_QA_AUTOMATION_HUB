## Check list: Cart

## Positive tests:
- Add a product to cart
- Delete product from the cart
- Proceed to checkout

## Negative tests:
- Add 999999 product count
- Put the wrong data to quantity of product (for example "qwdqwwdqwdwq")
- Put the number count of product below 0
- Proceed to checkout with empty cart
- Put the float number to quantity of product (1.5)
